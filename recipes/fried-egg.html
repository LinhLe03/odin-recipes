Fried egg

Description

Ingredients

  • 1 cup vegetable oil
  • 1 egg
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Heat the vegetable oil in a wok (or a shallow pan for frying) over medium-high heat until it gives off a few wisps of smoke.
  2. Once the oil is hot, crack an egg into the middle of the wok.
  3. Use a spatula to baste the hot oil over the egg. Do this for 1-2minutes or until the egg forms a crispy golden edge (or until the yolk is cooked to your preferred liking).
  4. Remove the egg from the wok. Drain on a cooling rack or paper towel and serve.
