Smashed avocado with milk

Description

In scorching summers, we all want something fresh to relieve the baking heat right? With Vietnamese, smashed avocados with sweet, fresh milk is one of our favourite ways to cool our bodies. Thus, this is an all-rounder in tropical, sunny countries.

Ingredients

  • 1 avocado
  • 2 spoons of condensed milk
  • 250ml of milk
  • shaved ice

Steps

  1. Prep

    2. Cut the avocado in half and take out its seed. Then either use a knife to peel its skin or a spoon to take out all the sweet avocado inside

  2. Mix everything!

    3. Put normal milk and condensed milk in a container, then put in avocado. After that, smash the avocado with a spoon to let sweetness come in

  3. Finish!

    4. Congrats! You have just created a wonderful beverage for summer! However, if you don't plan to eat them right now, put it in the fridge's cool compartment to maintain its flavour
